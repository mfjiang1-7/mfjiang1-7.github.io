Dr. Wang Ye, Associate Professor, PhD Supervisor, Member of CCF, Executive Member of the CCF Software Engineering Special Committee, and former Vice Chair of the Department of Software Engineering. He graduated from Zhejiang University with a major in Computer Science and Technology. His research mainly focuses on Intelligent Software Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, Natural Language Processing, and Service Computing. Dr. Wang has led National Natural Science Foundation projects, Zhejiang Provincial Natural Science Foundation projects, and Zhejiang's "14th Five-Year Plan" educational reform projects. He has also been a key member in several national and provincial research projects. He has published more than 40 papers in top-tier international and domestic journals and conferences, including 3 CCF A-level papers, 2 CCF Chinese A-level papers, 8 CCF B-level papers, and 3 JCR Q1 papers. He holds 5 authorized/published patents and has served as a reviewer for numerous international journals and conferences. Dr. Wang teaches the course "Software Modeling and Design," which has been awarded the "First-Class Course in Undergraduate Universities of Zhejiang Province." He has also contributed to the compilation of a key textbook for Zhejiang's "14th Five-Year Plan." In the past five years, he has supervised four undergraduate entrepreneurial projects, guiding students to win 7 awards in national and provincial competitions, including 2 National Second Prizes, 4 National Third Prizes, and 1 Provincial Third Prize.
