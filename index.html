About Me

Dr. Ye Wang

Ye Wang is an Associate Professor in the School of Computer Science and Technology at Zhejiang Gongshang University, a member of the China Computer Federation (CCF), and an executive committee member of the Software Engineering Special Committee. She graduated from Zhejiang University with a Bachlor in Software Engineering and PhD degree in Computer Science and Technology. Her main research areas include intelligent software engineering, artificial intelligence, natural language processing, and service computing. She has led projects funded by the National Natural Science Foundation of China, Zhejiang Provincial Natural Science Foundation, and the Tongxiang Artificial Intelligence Research Institute of Zhejiang Province. She has also been a key contributor to more than ten national and provincial-level research projects. She has published over 40 papers in journals such as IJHCS, SCIS, REJ, IST, IoT, JSEP and so on.
